Roza is a biological researcher in a prominent microbiology laboratory. She is trying to collect sample of bacerial species that have ability to synthesize proteins that taste like beef. Partnered with Improbable beef she has sampled the navels of people across the country to identify the bacterias in their belly buttons. Those who participated in the study have each been given a subject ID.
Based on the Subject ID:

  • The bar chart shows the top 10 bacterial species i.e. Operational Taxonomic Unit(OTU ID) found in each subject's belly buttons.
  • The gauge chart shows weekly washing frquency of their belly button.
  • The bubble chart, shows bacterial cultures per sample i.e. corresponding sample values(bacterial species name for each OTU ID) against the OTU ID.
